Here are some common problem specifications that can affect the various body parts :

Head:
- Headache/migraine
- Concussion
- Sinusitis
- Head injury

Eyes:
- Vision problems (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
- Eye infections (conjunctivitis)
- Dry eyes
- Glaucoma
- Cataracts
- Retinal detachment

Ears:
- Hearing loss
- Ear infections
- Tinnitus (ringing in the ears)
- Earwax blockage
- Eustachian tube dysfunction

Nose:
- Allergies
- Sinus infections
- Nasal congestion
- Deviated septum
- Nosebleeds

Mouth:
- Tooth decay
- Gum disease
- Bad breath (halitosis)
- Canker sores
- Temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorder

Neck:
- Neck pain
- Whiplash
- Cervical spondylosis
- Muscle strain
- Herniated disc

Shoulders:
- Shoulder impingement
- Rotator cuff tear
- Frozen shoulder
- Shoulder dislocation
- Shoulder bursitis

Arms:
- Tennis elbow (epicondylitis)
- Carpal tunnel syndrome
- Biceps tendinitis
- Arm fractures or sprains

Elbows:
- Golfer's elbow (medial epicondylitis)
- Olecranon bursitis
- Elbow dislocation
- Elbow fractures or sprains

Hands:
- Arthritis ( rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis)
- Carpal tunnel syndrome
- Dupuytren's contracture
- Trigger finger
- Hand fractures or sprains

Fingers:
- Finger fractures or sprains
- Trigger finger
- Raynaud's disease
- Finger infections (paronychia)

Chest:
- Chest pain
- Costochondritis (inflammation of the chest cartilage)
- Pectus excavatum (sunken chest)
- Rib fractures

Abdomen:
- Abdominal pain
- Gastroenteritis
- Appendicitis
- Hernia
- Gallbladder problems

Back:
- Lower back pain
- Sciatica
- Herniated disc
- Scoliosis
- Muscle strain

Hips:
- Hip arthritis
- Hip bursitis
- Hip labral tear
- Hip fractures
- Hip dislocation


Legs:
- Muscle strains or sprains
- Shin splints
- Restless legs syndrome
- Deep vein thrombosis (DVT)
- Peripheral artery disease (PAD)
- Varicose veins
- Leg cramps

Knees:
- Knee pain (e.g., osteoarthritis, patellofemoral pain syndrome)
- Knee ligament injuries (e.g., ACL tear, MCL tear)
- Meniscus tear
- Patellar tendinitis (jumper's knee)
- Bursitis (e.g., prepatellar bursitis, pes anserine bursitis)

Feet:
- Plantar fasciitis
- Achilles tendonitis
- Heel spurs
- Foot fractures or sprains
- Neuropathy (nerve damage)
- Bunions
- Ingrown toenails

Toes:
- Toe fractures or sprains
- Ingrown toenails
- Corns and calluses
- Gout (specifically affects the big toe)
- Hammertoe
- Morton's neuroma
