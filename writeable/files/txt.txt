Brain (মস্তিষ্ক):

Problems: Brain Tumor, Stroke, Epilepsy
Specifications: Abnormal cell growth in the brain, disruption of blood supply leading to cell damage, neurological disorder causing seizures.
Eyes (চোখ:):

Problems: Cataracts, Glaucoma, Conjunctivitis
Specifications: Clouding of the eye's lens, increased intraocular pressure, inflammation of the conjunctiva.
Ears (কান):

Problems: Hearing Loss, Tinnitus, Ear Infections
Specifications: Impairment of auditory function, ringing in the ears, inflammation or infection of the ear.
Nose (নাক):

Problems: Sinusitis, Allergic Rhinitis, Deviated Septum
Specifications: Inflammation of the sinuses, allergic reaction affecting the nasal passages, displacement of the nasal septum.
Mouth (মুখ):

Problems: Tooth Decay, Gum Disease, Oral Cancer
Specifications: Deterioration of tooth structure, inflammation of the gums, cancer affecting the oral cavity.
Throat structures (গলা প্রতিরক্ষা):

Problems: Pharyngitis, Laryngitis, Tonsillitis
Specifications: Inflammation of the pharynx, larynx, or tonsils.
Heart (হৃদয়):

Problems: Coronary Artery Disease, Heart Failure, Arrhythmia
Specifications: Narrowing of coronary arteries, weakened heart function, irregular heart rhythms.
Lungs (ফুসফুস):

Problems: Asthma, Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD), Pneumonia
Specifications: Chronic inflammation leading to airflow obstruction, respiratory condition causing difficulty in breathing.
Shoulder joint (কাঁধ জয়েন্ট):

Problems: Frozen Shoulder, Rotator Cuff Tear, Bursitis
Specifications: Restricted movement, tear in the rotator cuff muscles, inflammation of the shoulder bursa.
Upper arm muscles ( উপরের বাহুর পেশী):

Problems: Strain, Sprain, Tendonitis
Specifications: Overstretching or tearing of muscle fibers, inflammation of tendons.
Forearm muscles ( হাতের পেশী):

Problems: Tennis Elbow, Golfer's Elbow, Forearm Strain
Specifications: Inflammation of the tendons on the outer or inner side of the elbow, overuse or injury of forearm muscles.
Wrist joint (কব্জি জয়েন্ট):

Problems: Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, Wrist Sprain, Arthritis
Specifications: Compression of the median nerve, stretching or tearing of ligaments, inflammation of the wrist joint.
Hand bones and muscles (হাতের হাড় এবং পেশী):

Problems: Hand Fracture, Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, Trigger Finger
Specifications: Break in hand bones, compression of the median nerve, locking or catching of a finger tendon.
Hip joint (ঊরুসন্ধি):

Problems: Osteoarthritis, Hip Fracture, Bursitis
Specifications: Degeneration of joint cartilage, break in the hip bone, inflammation of the hip bursa.
